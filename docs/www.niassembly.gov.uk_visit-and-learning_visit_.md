# Visit Parliament Buildings
![Parliament Buildings pictured against a blue sky.](/globalassets/visit-and-tours/parlbldgs.jpg "Parliament Buildings in the Stormont Estate")
The Northern Ireland Assembly sits in Parliament Buildings.
### Where is Parliament Buildings?
Parliament Buildings in the Stormont Estate in Belfast.
### When is it open?
Parliament Buildings is open to the public from 9:00AM to 4:00PM, Monday to Friday except on public and bank holidays.
### Are there any guided tours?
Yes. Public tours are available Monday to Friday (excluding public and bank holidays). They are at 11:00AM and 2:00PM and you need to book a ticket. For more [information on public tours follow this link](/visit-and-learning/visit/tours/).
We also have a [video tour that you can watch by following this link](/visit-and-learning/visit/online-tour/ "Watch a video tour of Parliament Buildings").
 
---
### The Members' Dining Room
![A table in the Members' Dining Room of Parliament Buildings](/contentassets/ad69f6e921a0470d80678d878898cb10/diningrooma.jpg "The Members' Dining Room in Parliament Buildings")The **[Members’ Dining Room](/visit-and-learning/members-dining-room/)** is open to the public between Monday – Friday, 12.00pm-3:00pm (except Public Holidays).
All our food is prepared by talented and award winning chefs who are committed to using only locally available fresh produce and ingredients.
#### Booking is advisable. For reservations please call: 02890 521 041.
**Please note that the Members' Dining Room operates with a CASHLESS payment system.**
