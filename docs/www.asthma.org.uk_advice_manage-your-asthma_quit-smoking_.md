# 
 Stop smoking
Find out about different ways to quit smoking, how to cope with withdrawal symptoms, and when you’ll notice the benefits
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/Stop%20smoking%20-%20%20why%20is%20smoking%20bad%20for%20me.jpg?h=d1f56801&itok=FXZQV71i)
### 
 [Why is smoking bad for me?](/living-with/stop-smoking/why-is-it-bad)
 Find out why smoking is bad for your health and for people around you. We also explain why it can be hard to stop smoking and how inhaling any smoke is harmful.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/Stop%20smoking%20-%20%20How%20can%20I%20quit%20smoking.png?h=27fac3b4&itok=9_TeIzN_)
### 
 [How can I quit smoking](/living-with/stop-smoking/quit)
 You’re more likely to quit smoking if you have help from friends, family, and professionals. Find out about the different ways to help you quit smoking.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/Stop%20smoking%20-%20%20What%20is%20vaping.jpg?h=b3a09ea2&itok=Gjkvpi_c)
### 
 [Vaping and e-cigarettes](/living-with/stop-smoking/vaping)
 Find out how e-cigarettes can be a useful tool to help you quit smoking.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/Stop%20smoking%20-%20%20Withdrawal%20symptoms.png?h=2145bc02&itok=wRnCqHzm)
### 
 [Withdrawal symptoms](/living-with/stop-smoking/withdrawal)
 Find out how to deal with withdrawal symptoms after stopping smoking. We have information on ways to cope and what to do if you start smoking again.
