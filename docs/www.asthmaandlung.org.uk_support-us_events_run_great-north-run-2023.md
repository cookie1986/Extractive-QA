# Great North Run 2023
Join Team Breathe for the biggest half marathon in the world!
10th September 2023
Newcastle
13.1 miles
Registration fee £35
Minimum sponsorship £350
## About the event
The Great North Run will be following its iconic city to sea course. Experience its unbeatable atmosphere alongside 57,000 runners – and do your bit for people with lung conditions.
### Charity place
Sign up directly through Asthma + Lung UK. Pay a registration fee, raise a minimum of £350 in sponsorship and be a part of  #TeamBreathe!
[Get your charity place](https://endurancecui.active.com/new/events/82942034/select-race?error=login_required&state=32ac494a-6e6e-4638-82c0-28fc455aef24&_p=13924385765845138)
### Secured your own place?
There’s no fundraising target when you join #TeamBreathe with your own place, but you’ll still receive a VIP experience along with our charity place runners!
[Join our team now](https://endurancecui.active.com/new/events/82982632/select-race?error=login_required&state=e7b46800-420e-4b57-bd7a-131cdf162e12&_p=6800917406621925)
### What you'll get:
* A personalised running vest
* Advice from our expert running coaches
* The loudest cheer points on the big day
* An after party with a free massage and a glass of fizz!
If you have any questions about the event, simply contact our events team at [events@asthmaandlung.org.uk](mailto:events@asthmaandlung.org.uk).
