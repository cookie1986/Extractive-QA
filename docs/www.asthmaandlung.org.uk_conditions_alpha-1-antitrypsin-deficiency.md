# Alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency
This information is for people in the UK who have been diagnosed with alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency, their families, friends and carers. Alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency is also known as AATD or A1AD.
## 
 About Alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency
* ![Asthma + Lung UK](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-01/aluk_0.png?h=ec041e41&itok=cRRgMdwz)
### 
 [What is alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency?](/conditions/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency/what-alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency)
 
 Alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency (A1ATD) is a rare, inherited condition that affects the lungs. Find out more about who is affected
* ![Asthma + Lung UK](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-01/aluk_0.png?h=ec041e41&itok=cRRgMdwz)
### 
 [What is the cause of alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency?](/conditions/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency/what-cause-alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency)
 
 Learn about the symptoms of alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency (AATD), an inherited condition that affects the lungs.
* ![Asthma + Lung UK](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-01/aluk_0.png?h=ec041e41&itok=cRRgMdwz)
### 
 [What health problems can alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency cause?](/conditions/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency/what-health-problems-can-alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency)
 
 AATD doesn’t directly cause symptoms. Some people with AATD experience no symptoms at all.
* ![Asthma + Lung UK](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-01/aluk_0.png?h=ec041e41&itok=cRRgMdwz)
### 
 [How is alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency diagnosed?](/conditions/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency/how-alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency-diagnosed)
 
 Find out when you should seek medical advice for alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency symptoms, and what to expect from tests for AATD.
* ![Asthma + Lung UK](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-01/aluk_0.png?h=ec041e41&itok=cRRgMdwz)
### 
 [Treatment for alpha-1-antitrypsin deficiency](/conditions/alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency/treatment-alpha-1-antitrypsin-deficiency)
 
 There is no approved treatment or cure for AATD in the UK, but you can follow our advice to help manage your symptoms.
