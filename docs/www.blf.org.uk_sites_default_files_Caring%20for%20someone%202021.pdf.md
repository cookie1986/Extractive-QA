# Caring for someone with a lung condition
In this information, we give practical help for people caring for someone with a lung condition - from supporting the person you care for, to looking after yourself. We also provide tips and stories from other carers.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/holding%20hands%20support%20care.jpg?h=199d8c1f&itok=4TQGYWfH)
### 
 [Who is a carer?](/living-with/caring/who)
 Find out what it means to be a carer and what your situation may involve.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/anne_brown_caring_shopping_2016.jpg?h=f0370f5f&itok=gTq-70bT)
### 
 [Tips from other carers](/living-with/caring/tips)
 Caring for someone with a lung condition? We've talked to people who are, or have been, carers. Read about their experiences and their top tips for caring for someone with a lung condition.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/InMemory_OlderCouple_1.jpg?h=1560f194&itok=sV8JAXSP)
### 
 [How can I help the person I care for?](/living-with/caring/help)
 Find out what you can do to help the person you care for, whether that’s meeting their needs or understanding their condition.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/2023-03/3_severe_asthma.jpg?h=c31668e0&itok=MKXTmKy_)
### 
 [Support for carers](/living-with/caring/support)
 It’s so important as a carer to take time for yourself. In this section, we talk about how you can look after yourself, whether that’s your health, work and life balance, or your finances.
