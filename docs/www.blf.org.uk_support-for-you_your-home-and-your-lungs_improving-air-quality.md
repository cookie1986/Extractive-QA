# Indoor air pollution
Find out about the types of air pollution you might find in your home. We also have advice on how to manage allergies caused by indoor pollution at home 
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/cleaning%20decorating%20vocs%20lungs%202016.jpg?h=a383c038&itok=HnRORVFk)
### 
 [Air pollution at home](/living-with/indoor-air-pollution/home)
 Find out the common causes of air pollution at home, from the cleaning products you use, to the way you heat your home.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/man%20with%20cat%20allergy%202016%20400.jpg?h=066d1de8&itok=8ZR8rAIm)
### 
 [Allergies at home](/living-with/indoor-air-pollution/allergies)
 Find out about the different allergies you may have at home. From dust mites and cockroaches, to pets, damp, and mould.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/man-sitting-by-open-window-at-home-2016.jpg?h=afb94278&itok=5RW0RyiW)
### 
 [Improving indoor air quality](/living-with/indoor-air-pollution/improving)
 Use our handy checklist to find out the best ways to prevent indoor air pollution at home. You can also find out what to do if your symptoms are not improving.
* ![](/sites/default/files/styles/listing_image_mobile_x1/public/construction-site-workers.jpg?h=df1f1a57&itok=9EDicdL_)
### 
 [Air pollution at work](/living-with/indoor-air-pollution/work)
 We have information to help you speak to colleagues and your employer about air pollution at work. We also have information about asthmagens.
