 Sotrovimab (Xevudy)
Sotrovimab is no longer widely used to treat coronavirus (COVID-19).
It may be used in some circumstances but [Paxlovid](/medicines/paxlovid/), [remdesivir](/medicines/remdesivir-veklury/) and [molnupiravir](/medicines/molnupiravir/) are usually used instead.
