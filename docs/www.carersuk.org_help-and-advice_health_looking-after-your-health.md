# Looking after yourself
 
Scroll down
 
**Caring for someone can be rewarding but it can also be very tiring, putting a lot of demands on your physical and emotional energy. When you are simply coping day to day and responding to the needs of others, it's easy to forget your own health needs.** **Here are some key topics to help you.**
