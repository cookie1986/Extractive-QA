# Our partners
Thousands of people with lung conditions are improving their quality of life, thanks to the sponsorship, time and skills of our corporate partners.
Our corporate partners can get involved in a variety of ways, such as supporting our [Taskforce for Lung Health](https://www.blf.org.uk/taskforce), arranging workplace lung testing events, sponsoring health information or [health care professional study days](https://www.blf.org.uk/health-care-professionals/study-days).
If you’re thinking of partnering with us, see who you’ll be joining in the fight against lung disease.
