# We're on Social Media!
Our social media channels share news, photos, and resources with you about what’s happening at the Northern Ireland Assembly and how you can get involved. You can follow the work of the Assembly and its Committees on:
* [Twitter](https://twitter.com/niassembly "Follow us @niassembly") – keep up to date with what's happening
* [Facebook](http://facebook.com/northernirelandassembly) – like the Assembly page on facebook
* [Youtube](http://www.youtube.com/theniassembly "Youtube - videos about the NI Assembly") – videos of Plenary sessions and Committee meetings
* [Instagram](http://instagram.com/niassembly) – Pictures, videos and reels
* [Flickr](http://www.flickr.com/niassembly "Flickr - images from the NI Assembly") – images of events and activities inside and outside the Assembly
We welcome all your questions, comments, feedback, and photos. We also we encourage respectful discussion. You don't have to agree with what other people say or share, but please treat everybody with dignity, courtesy, and respect.You can read more about how we use social media in our [Social Media Guidelines](/news-and-media/social-media/social-media-community-guidelines/ "Social Media Guidelines for the Northern Ireland Assembly") or find a list of all [Assembly Social Media Accounts](/news-and-media/social-media/social-media-community-guidelines/ "A list of all Northern Ireland Assembly Social Media Accounts").
