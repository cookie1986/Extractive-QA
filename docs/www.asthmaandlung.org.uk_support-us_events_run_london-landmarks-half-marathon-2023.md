# London Landmarks Half Marathon 2023
The London Landmarks Half Marathon winds through the London's most famous sights, celebrating our capital’s amazing, fun and quirky history. The party atmosphere from the cheer points around the route will make it a day to remember!
2nd April 2023
13.1 miles
Registration fee £35
Minimum sponsorship £350
